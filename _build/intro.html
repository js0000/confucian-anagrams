Confucian Anagrams ========= ======== This is a project to take the Analects of Confucious and turn them into something else. It may end up as poetry or it may end up as nonsense- that is up to you more than I.

It is a project investigating how an ancient text can be modified to fit better within our modern cultural world. Having an anti-authoritarian nature, there are many aspects of the Confucian world view that are not the same as mine. Nonetheless, I do not wish to ridicule the Analects, instead I hope to find a way to pass them through some automated filters to come up with something respectful interesting and wholly unexpected.

It’s very easy to have high expectations at the start of a project.
